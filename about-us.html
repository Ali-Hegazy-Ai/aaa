About Us

Discover the passion and innovation behind Egyptian Italian Date Liquid Sugar

Who We Are

Egyptian Italian Date Liquid Sugar (EIDLS) is the only manufacturer in Egypt and the leading one in Africa specializing in date liquid sugar products. Founded with a vision to transform premium Egyptian dates into innovative, healthy alternatives to refined sugar, we have grown to become pioneers in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art factory currently covers 10,000 square meters and is expanding to 40,000 square meters to meet growing global demand. We combine traditional knowledge with cutting-edge technology to create products that preserve the natural goodness of dates while offering exceptional flavor and nutritional benefits.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, natural date products that offer healthier alternatives to refined sugar while supporting sustainable agriculture and economic development in Egypt.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in date-based sweeteners, recognized worldwide for innovation, quality, and sustainability, while promoting the unique properties of Egyptian dates.
